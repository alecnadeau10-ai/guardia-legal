Privacy Policy — Guardia

Last updated: 2026-06-12 · Version française

1. Who we are

Guardia is a mobile app that helps you get back the money you're owed: it tracks return windows, warranties and price adjustments from your purchase receipts. Contact: contact@reseau-emergence.ca.

2. Data we collect

  • Account: e-mail address (or Google / Apple identifier if you use social sign-in).
  • Receipts: photos of receipts you scan, imported PDF invoices, and the extracted data (merchant, date, amount, items, warranties).
  • E-mails (optional, with your explicit authorization): if you connect Gmail or Outlook, we read receipt-like messages in read-only mode to extract purchase details. E-mails themselves are never stored — only the extracted receipt data and, where present, the attached PDF invoice are kept.
  • Subscription: subscription status managed by RevenueCat and the App Store (we never see your payment information).

3. AI processing

Receipt data extraction uses AI models (Google Gemini and/or Anthropic Claude). Receipt images and text are sent to these services solely for extraction, under their terms; they are not used to train models. AI-estimated warranty durations are clearly flagged in the app.

4. Where your data lives

Your data is hosted on Supabase (AWS infrastructure, US West region), encrypted at rest and in transit. Receipt images and PDFs live in private storage accessible only by your account.

5. Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. It is shared only with the technical processors required to operate the service: Supabase (hosting), Google Gemini / Anthropic (AI extraction), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Google / Microsoft (if you connect your mailbox).

6. Your rights and deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all your data directly in the app: Settings → Delete my account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible (account, receipts, images, import data). You can also revoke Gmail/Outlook access at any time from your Google/Microsoft security settings. For any request (access, rectification, portability): contact@reseau-emergence.ca.

7. Retention

Data is kept for as long as your account exists. After account deletion, all data is removed from our active systems immediately.

8. Changes

Any change to this policy will be published on this page with a new update date.
