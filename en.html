Features

Every item gets its own return deadline, based on the merchant's policy. Guardia reminds you 7, 3 and 1 days before it expires — nothing slips by in silence.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Guardia really free?

Yes. The free version lets you track up to 5 active items at a time, with AI scanning, return and warranty tracking, and alerts. Guardia Pro ($2.99/month or $24.99/year) removes the limit and adds automatic email import and web-verified warranties.

How does receipt scanning work?

You take a photo of your receipt (or import it from your gallery). Vision AI extracts the merchant, date, items and amounts, then you review the result before saving. If you prefer, you can also enter everything manually.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your account is protected (sign in with email, Google or Apple), your data is encrypted in transit and stored on secure infrastructure where every account is strictly isolated. Your receipts are yours: they are never sold or shared. The details are in our privacy policy.

Which devices is Guardia available on?

Guardia is available on iPhone via the App Store, in English and French. An Android version is in the works — coming soon to the Play Store.

Does Guardia handle claims for me?

Guardia prepares everything so a claim takes you two minutes: tracked deadlines, a pre-written claim email, a PDF proof of purchase. The final send stays in your hands — you press “send”, so you keep full control of your communications.
